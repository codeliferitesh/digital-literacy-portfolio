Email Etiquette Checklist:

- Use a clear and relevant subject line
- Start with a polite greeting (Dear Sir/Madam)
- Maintain formal and professional language
- Keep the email short and to the point
- Avoid slang or informal words
- Use proper grammar and punctuation
- Clearly state your purpose
- End with a polite closing (Thank you / Yours sincerely)
- Include your name and details
- Proofread before sending
